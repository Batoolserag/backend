About GPS Club

Who We Are

GPS Club (Global Public Speaking Club) is an online platform dedicated to helping individuals grow into confident, impactful speakers. Through interactive features, community support, and real–world practice, we empower members to speak with clarity, purpose, and presence.

What We Offer

At GPS Club, we offer a supportive space to grow as a speaker. Members can submit speeches, receive constructive feedback, track their progress with badges and analytics, join global contests and events, and improve with the help of AI–powered coaching and curated resources.

Why It Matters

At GPS Club, we believe that public speaking is more than just a skill — it’s a pathway to confidence, connection, and leadership. Every voice has power, and every story deserves to be heard.

Meet the Team

We’re a passionate, diverse group working together to make GPS Club the best it can be.
